Hotel visitors
==============

You are given check in and check out dates of visitors in
a hotel. Find our maximal number of people present in the
hotel during a day.

Example
```
Input: [(1, 2), (1, 3), (2, 4), (2, 3)]
Output: 4
In second day there were 4 people in the hotel. Iow

           1 2 3 4 day
visitor 1 |-|-|
visitor 2 |-|-|-|
visitor 3   |-|-|-|
visitor 4   |-|-|

```
